Vision

So many women are transformed and inspired by the Seminary experience only to enter back into demanding schedules of college and work. Many do not want to have to choose between camp, summer school, or a Beis Medrash program. Some people would like to learn but can't travel far from home. For all those reasons The Summernary provides a great opportunity to be inspired again by the Seminary experience without needing it to be all or nothing.

The Summernary is being run by experienced Rebbeim who have all previously taught in various summer learning programs.
