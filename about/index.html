Teachers

Rav Gershon Turetsky

Rav Gershon Turetsky has been a member of the Sha'alvim For Women faculty since the school’s inception. He received a B.S. from Yeshiva University. Subsequently, he learned in Yeshiva University's Kollel as part of the Semicha program in New York and in Jerusalem. Additionally, he was a member of Yeshivat Sha'alvim’s Kollel where he completed his Semicha studies and received ordination from HaRav Zalman Nechemia Goldberg of Jerusalem. For a number of summers he has served as the Director of Camp Morasha’s girls Beit Medrash Program (BMP).

Rav Moshe Tzvi Weinberg

Rav Moshe Tzvi Weinberg currently teaches in Yeshiva University’s Irving I. Stone Beit Midrash Program (SBMP), where he also he serves as Mashgiach Ruchani. A graduate of Yeshiva University, Rav Weinberg received his semicha from HaRav Zalman Nechemia Goldberg of Jerusalem and has also been certified by the Center for Integrated Teacher Education. Additionally, Rav Weinberg serves as Mashpia at Congregation Beth Abraham in Bergenfield, N.J. where he lives with his family.

Rav Rafi Rosenblum

Rav Rafi Rosenblum earned a BA and semichah at Yeshiva University, where he also spent four years as a member of the Wexner Kollel Elyon. After making aliyah to Ramat Bet Shemesh in 2012, he started teaching at a number of seminaries and yeshivos. Currently, Rav Rafi teaches halacha and hashkafa at Midreshet Amit, Midreshet Moriah, and Sha’alvim For Women, where his classes are popular for their interactive nature and exposure to new halachic topics.
