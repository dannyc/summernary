Q: I really want to join the program, but I can't afford to pay that much money. Is there anything I can do?

A: No one will be turned away due to funding. Please contact us to work something out.

Q: Do you need to be on a certain learning level to join the Summernary?

A: We are pleased to be able to offer shiurim on a number of levels. In addition, we will have a teacher available to answer questions that come up during chavruta learning to help figure things out.

Q: Will there be swag?

A: YES!

Q: Will the Rebbeim be around all day?

A: Rabbi Turetsky, Rav Moshe Tzvi, and Rav Rafi plan on being around from the start of the day until the wee hours of the night (or whenever people go home). In addition to giving shiurim and answering questions in the Beit Midrash, we will be around to shmooze at lunch time and after night seder is over.

Q: What is included in night seder?

A: The night seder program will offer us an opportunity to hear from some of the best teachers and speakers in America. We are in the process of arranging an amazing list of guest speakers. As soon as we finalize everything, we will publish a list of speakers.

Q: What is included in the program?

A: In addition to the learning, we are planning on providing lunch, an awesome Shabbaton, swag, a trip, and a סעודת פרידה.

Q: I would like to sponsor a day of learning - is that a possibility?

A: If you would like to sponsor a day of learning לעילוי נשמת someone or in someone's honor (or any other occasion), please contact us. We will announce in the beginning of the day that the learning is in honor of ...
