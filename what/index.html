Each Seder will have an option of captivating and inspiring Shiurim or classic in depth "Beit Medrash" style learning. One of esteemed Rabbanim will present in the Beit Medrash throughout the day

This summer, our main focus is going to be on the three weeks. We plan on covering the halachot of the three weeks and also the hashkafa of galut and geulah. We would also like to offer mini refresher courses (some options include hilchot brachot, hilchot kashrut, hilchot tefillah, hilchot lashon hara, hilchot kibud av v'em).
